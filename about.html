Our Story || IMMCO

IMMCO, Inc. was founded in 1992 in order to utilize the growing capabilities of the Internet towards the cable industry. The company quickly became a leading provider of enterprise solutions to the cable & telecom industries with specialization in broadband, geospatial services, and software development. IMMCO currently has a large pool of employees across 4 offices in India and the United States of America. IMMCO is headquartered in Alpharetta [Atlanta], Georgia and is supported by teams spanning expertise software, engineering, business, and telecom.

IMMCO is the 2012 recipient of the Bentley’s prestigious Be Inspired Award for Innovation in Communications Networks for their development of an Automated Quality Control Toolkit

IMMCO's mission is to deliver quality work for customers while creating innovative solutions to solve market challenges. Our work in the broadband, telecommunications, and cable industries delivers essential technologies to millions of households across the world. We strive to exude our core values of integrity, resourcefulness, and excellence.
    - Ramesh Iyer Ph.D, Founder & President
